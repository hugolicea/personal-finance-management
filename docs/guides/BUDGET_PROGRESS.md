# Budget Progress

Budget Progress shows how much you've spent in each spending category this
month. One look at the page and you know which categories are fine, which ones
are getting close, and which you've already gone over.

---

## When to Use

Open this page mid-month or at the end of the month to check where your money is
going. It answers one question: "am I on track this month?"

---

## How to Open Budget Progress

1. Open the sidebar.
2. Click **Budget Progress** (the target icon in the sidebar, right below
   Dashboard).

---

## What You'll See

The page shows one card per spending category, sorted alphabetically. Each card
shows:

- The category name
- How much you've spent this month
- The budget limit you set
- A color-coded progress bar

**The bar changes color as you spend:**

- **Green**: under 75% of your budget used. You're in good shape.
- **Yellow**: 75% to 99% used. Worth paying attention.
- **Red**: at or over 100%. The card gets a red border on the left and a pulsing
  **Over Budget** badge.

At the bottom of each card:

- **Under budget**: shows how much you have left (e.g. "$150.00 left")
- **Over budget**: shows how much you've gone over (e.g. "$50.00 over budget")
- **No budget set**: shows "No budget set" in gray. The bar stays empty and no
  percentage appears.

Income categories never appear on this page. Budget Progress is for spending
only.

---

## Example

You set a $300 budget for Groceries. By mid-month you've spent $240. The card
shows a yellow bar and "$60.00 left."

You spend another $90. The bar turns red, the card shows a pulsing **Over
Budget** badge, and the footer reads "$30.00 over budget." Time to revisit that
grocery list.

---

## Tips and Common Mistakes

**No categories appear on the page.** The page shows a message with a link to
the Categories page. You need to add spending categories there before anything
shows up here.

**A card says "No budget set".** You can't edit budgets from this page. Go to
the Categories page, set a limit there, then come back. The card will update.

**You want to check a past month.** This page only shows the current month.
There is no way to view past months from here.

**Your income categories are missing.** That's expected. Budget Progress shows
spending categories only. Income categories are never listed here.

**The page shows an error.** Try refreshing. If it keeps happening, check your
internet connection.
